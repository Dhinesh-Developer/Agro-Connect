About Agro Connect

Digital Marketplace for Farmers: This platform enables farmers to directly connect with buyers, wholesalers, and local markets. Farmers can list their produce, set prices, and negotiate deals, cutting out middlemen and ensuring better profit margins.

Financial Literacy & Support Hub: The platform will include a Financial Literacy and Support Hub, providing farmers with easy-to-understand information on managing their finances, accessing government subsidies, and applying for loans or insurance.

Integrated Payment Gateway: Agro Connect will feature a secure payment gateway that facilitates transactions between farmers and buyers. Farmers can receive payments directly into their bank accounts or mobile wallets, ensuring quick and safe transactions.
